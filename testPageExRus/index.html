What is a Coding Champion?

A Coding Champion is a parent who, like us, believes that learning to code should be an essential part of every child’s development. They believe technology is the future. They believe that it’s our responsibility to provide opportunities for our kids to develop the skills they’ll need in an environment built for learning.

A Coding Champion is an advocate for bringing coding programs into schools so more kids can be exposed to computer programming, robotics, and technology education.

Want to learn how you can partner with CodeAdvantage to advance coding education in your child’s school and beyond? Fill out our contact form or send us an email. We’d love to hear from you.

Why become a Coding Champion?

At CodeAdvantage we are, first and foremost, parents just like you. We believe that all kids should have access to coding programs from an early age.

With help from parents who share our passion, we’ve been able to teach STEM programs to thousands of children by being asked to bring our programs into top public schools, private schools, and after-school programs in the NYC/NJ metro area.

As a thank you, if you become a Coding Champion and help bring CodeAdvantage to your school, we offer free classes for your child.
